Travel Tips

🗓️
Best Time to Visit

Plan between November to February to enjoy cool highland weather and blooming flowers.

🚌
Getting There

Take a Victory Liner or Joy Bus from Manila to Baguio — the main gateway to all Benguet municipalities.

🛡️
Safety & Hiking

Always hire DOT-accredited local guides for treks like Mt. Pulag for your safety and environmental protection.

🌿
Responsible Tourism

Practice Leave No Trace principles. Benguet is a sacred highland — respect local customs and bring your trash back.

🧥
What to Bring

Pack warm clothing, a light rain jacket, and sunscreen. Weather can change rapidly in the mountains.

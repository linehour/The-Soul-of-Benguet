About Benguet

Our Roots & Identity

LocationSouthernmost part of the Cordillera Administrative Region (CAR).
BackgroundA landlocked highland province officially established in 1966, serving as the gateway to the most majestic Cordillera peaks.
CultureRich living heritage sustained by the indigenous Ibaloi, Kankanaey, and Kalanguya tribes.
CapitalLa Trinidad — known as the Strawberry Capital of the Philippines.
Why Visit Benguet?

"It offers a peaceful escape where everything slows down, with cool mountain air and quiet surroundings to recharge your soul."

A Legacy of Beauty

Benguet serves as a sanctuary for those seeking adventure and tranquility. From the highest peaks of Mt. Pulag to the terraced gardens of La Trinidad, every corner tells a story of resilience and natural wonder.

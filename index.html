Highland Roots

Benguet is a landlocked highland province known for its majestic canyons and ridges. Bordered by Mountain Province to the north and Pangasinan to the south, it is composed of 13 municipalities and 140 barangays.

Provincial Symbolism

Approved in 1968, the seal symbolizes Unity, Industry, and Prosperity. It features the traditional "gangsa" and tools representing the mining and agricultural foundations of Benguet province.
